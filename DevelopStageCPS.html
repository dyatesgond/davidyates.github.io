Stage 3: Developing Your Most Promising Ideas into Viable Solutions





A. FRAMING YOUR SOLUTION(S) AS A STORY

  1. Review your themed clusters of ideas and blend them into a story.
  2. Imagine in detail what your solution will look like when it is implemented.
  3. Begin your solution story with the phrase What I see myself doing is...

B. THE PPCo TOOL

P= Pluses (current strengths or advantages)

P= Potentials (future strengths or advantages)

C= Concerns (phrased as positive questions beginning with How might I..., or What might be all the ways..., or How to...)

O= Ways of Overcoming the concerns (phrased as short statements beginning with action verbs)

C. USING PPCo TO EVALUATE & DEVELOP YOUR SOLUTION STORY
  1. List 3 plusses or specific current strengths of your solution Story.
  2. List 3 potentials of your solution Story. If you bring it to fruition, what might be positive outcomes?
  3. List 3 concerns about your solution Story, all of then phrased as positive questions beginning with How might I..., >What might be all the ways..., or How to...

    4. Diverge

  4. Use the Brainstorming Tool to Generate action steps for overcoming each concern, one concern at a time. Action steps should be short statements that start with verbs.

    5. Converge

  5. Use the Highlightig Tool to select the most promising of the action steps for overcoming each concern to improve your solution
  6. Rephrase each of your concerns beginning with the words In order to....
  7. Beneath each of these In order to... statements, write I will...
  8. Move the promising action steps you selected in Step 8 next to the appropriate I will... statements.
