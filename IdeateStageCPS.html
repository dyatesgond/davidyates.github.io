Stage 2: Generate Ideas for Achieving the Goal, Realizing the Wish, Meeting the Challenge, or Solving the Problem

    Ideate Stage Guidelines

  • Start here when you have a clearly defined goal, wish, challenge or problem.
  • The best way to create great ideas is to generate LOTS of ideas.
  • Defer Judgment when generating ideas. This is NOT the time to evaluate any idea.
  • Seek wild and unusual ideas. No idea is too crazy or too impractical.
  • Build on the ideas of others or use pictures, objects, sounds, and smells to make connections.

    A. GENERATE IDEAS

    Diverge:

  1. Use divergent thinking tools like Brainstorming, Brainwriting, Idea Box, Forced Connections, or Excursions.
    2. Come up with at least 30-40 ideas and keep going as long as time permits.
  2. Don't stop just because you have some promising ideas.
  3. Stretch and keep pushing for novelty.
  4. Remember not to judge any ideas in this part of the CPS process.

    B. SELECT THE BEST IDEAS

    Converge:

  1. Use convergent thinking tools like Highlighting, Card Sort,Evaluation Matrix, or Targeting.
  2. Mark the Hits (Ideas that are particularly interesting, intriguing, novel or useful.)
  3. Group the related Hits together in clusters.
  4. Label each cluster as an action statement that begins with a verb and captures the overall theme of the cluster.
